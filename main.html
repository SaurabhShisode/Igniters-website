D2C Igniters Club is a tech-focused club at VIT Bhopal, dedicated to fostering innovation, creativity, and hands-on learning in technology. The club provides a platform for students to explore various domains of tech, including software development, AI, robotics, and cybersecurity, through hackathons, workshops, and fun tech-based events.
